**Key Insights & Findings**
1. Revenue Performance
Current Standing: The organization generated 19.16M in total sales, matching exactly with the previous year's performance (0.00% growth). This suggests a stabilized market presence but highlights a need for new growth strategies.
Peak Seasonality: Sales follow a strong upward trajectory in the first half of the year, reaching a peak in June ($2.1M). There is a notable "summer slump" in July, followed by a steady recovery through Q4.
2. Product Mix & Dominance
Category Leaders: The inventory is heavily skewed toward Clothing, which accounts for a massive 97.79% of total sales.
Opportunity Areas: Accessories and Bikes represent less than 3% of the total share, indicating a potential opportunity to diversify marketing efforts or investigate supply chain constraints in these categories.
3. Operational Efficiency
Order Correlation: Monthly Order Quantity closely mirrors the Sales Trend, peaking in June. This indicates that revenue growth is driven by transaction volume rather than just higher-priced item sales.
Customer Engagement: With 6,954 customers placing 9,400 orders, the average customer frequency is approximately 1.35 orders per person, suggesting a healthy mix of new and returning buyers.
4. Geographical Market Share
Top Markets: Australia ($6.4M) and the United States ($5.9M) are the primary revenue drivers, together making up the majority of the global portfolio.
European Presence: The UK, Germany, and France show consistent performance (averaging ~$1.8M each), representing stable secondary markets.
Technical Highlights
Bilingual Interface: Includes English and Arabic localization for global stakeholder accessibility.
Time-Series Analysis: Features a dynamic date slicer for customized reporting periods.
Cross-Page Navigation: Organized into three core sections: Sales Overview, Customer/Promotion Insights, and detailed reports.
