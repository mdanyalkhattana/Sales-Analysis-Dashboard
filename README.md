# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard – Insights & Findings

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer behavior, product contribution, and regional market share. It is designed with a bilingual interface (English & Arabic) and supports dynamic time-series reporting.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Findings

### 1. Revenue Performance

- **Current Standing:**  
  The organization generated **$19.16M** in total sales, matching exactly with the previous year's performance (**0.00% growth**).  
  This indicates a **stable market presence**, while also highlighting the need for **new growth strategies**.

- **Peak Seasonality:**  
  Sales show a strong upward trend during the **first half of the year**, reaching a peak in **June ($2.1M)**.  
  A noticeable **summer slump occurs in July**, followed by a **steady recovery through Q4**.

---

### 2. Product Mix & Dominance

- **Category Leaders:**  
  The inventory is heavily dominated by **Clothing**, which contributes **97.79% of total sales**.

- **Opportunity Areas:**  
  **Accessories** and **Bikes** contribute **less than 3%** of total sales.  
  This suggests potential opportunities to:
  - diversify marketing campaigns
  - optimize product mix
  - investigate possible supply chain constraints
